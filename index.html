Sleeping With Sirens is a rock band with pop-punk influences. The band was formed in 2009 and consists of five members: Kellin Quinn, Tony Pizzuti, Nick Martin, Justin Hills and Matty Best. The meaning behind the bands name is described by lead vocalist Quinn: "We knew we were going to create 'watery' music. You know, hardcore music with a pretty voice."
